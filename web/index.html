Interview pitch

What to say in the meeting

I prepared a small project concept called ThermoSplice Rice AI. The idea is to study how rice responds to heat stress by integrating RNA-seq expression data, alternative-splicing changes, and recovery or temperature-memory signals. My goal would be to rank candidate genes or splicing events that may contribute to thermotolerance, then use those candidates for biological validation.
