# Professor Profile: Prof. Dr. Cristiane Paula Gomes Calixto

## Official University Profile Summary

Prof. Dr. Cristiane Paula Gomes Calixto is listed by the Institute of Biosciences, University of São Paulo (IB-USP), as a faculty member in the Department of Botany.

The Postgraduate Botany faculty page at IB-USP describes her group as follows:

> Nosso grupo visa compreender a complexidade da regulação da expressão gênica de plantas em resposta a estresses abióticos, integrando mecanismos pós-transcricionais e epigenéticos.

English meaning:

Her group aims to understand the complexity of plant gene-expression regulation in response to abiotic stresses, integrating post-transcriptional and epigenetic mechanisms.
